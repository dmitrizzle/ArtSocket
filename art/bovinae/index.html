Bovinae

An incredible sight of the warm California sun grazing the grass hills and the cattle roaming them followed me during my drive from San Francisco to Los Angles. I stopped for a break with my camera and snapped a quick photo. The vista looked unreal under the evening lights. So I composed this image to look like a 17th century painting (flat, with limited colors) to capture that fairy-tale feeling.
